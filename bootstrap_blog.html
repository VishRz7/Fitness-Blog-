Importance of exercises


1. Physical Fitness

Sports not only help you stay healthy, but also help you stay healthy. Regular exercise promotes your overall health, which boosts your immune system and makes you more resistant to illnesses such as the common cold. When combined with regular vaccinations, exercise can be a ticket to survive the flu season.

2. Mental health

Exercise has been shown to have a mood-boosting effect. This is because it can release chemicals into the brain to make you feel better and reduce the effects of depression, ADHD, and anxiety. It can also allow you to sleep better at night, and it is important to maintain a good mood overall.

3. Weight Management

One of the most common benefits of exercise is that it helps you manage and control your weight. It burns calories, resulting in the shedding of pounds. Hard exercise burns more calories, but even simple exercise, such as an active walk, can have a positive effect on weight loss.

4. Long Term Health

In addition to all the direct benefits of regular exercise, it can help you prevent long-term health problems like heart disease and diabetes. Exercise increases your "good" cholesterol and reduces the risk of serious medical problems, especially those related to the heart. Helps a variety of health conditions and is as easy as a walk. Try incorporating physical activity into your daily life little by little!

Types of exercises

April 4, 2022
1. Flexible Exercise

Flexible exercises include stretch exercises such as back stretch, inner thigh stretch, and ankle stretch

2. Strength Exercises

These are strength training such as climbing stairs, lifting weights, and carrying groceries.

3. Balance Exercises

These are lower body strength workouts that help improve balance. Exercises include standing on one leg, heel-toe

4. Endurance Exercises

These exercises are often aerobic exercises that help increase breathing and heart rate
